About

Let me introduce myself

I’m Sam, a front-end developer and designer. After working as a graphic designer in the printing industry for many years, I became interested in designing and building websites. What started as a simple curiosity led me to gain the knowledge and experience necessary to totally pivot my career. Now I’m a lead front-end web developer with a passion for creative design that is both visually pleasing and functional.

My day-to-day typically consists of writing semantic Liquid HTML and responsive SCSS as well as digesting JSON data from ajax requests and writing JavaScript to build more dynamic pages. As front-end lead, I’m also responsible for all changes being pushed to our themes. I review code, perform QA, and manage pull requests conducting weekly theme releases. I’ve also developed and refined the necessary procedures to enable a seamless front-end development process.
